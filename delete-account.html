FAVOUR – Account Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your FAVOUR account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account, please email us using the details below.

  • Email: favour.33@hotmail.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Include: The email address or user ID associated with your FAVOUR account

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account and authentication record
  • Your profile information
  • Your favour requests and help offers
  • Your messages and related user content

Timeframe

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. Some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at:

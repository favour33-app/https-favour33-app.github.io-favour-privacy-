FAVOUR – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 23 January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how FAVOUR collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

  • Account information (e.g., email address, user ID) when you sign in
  • Profile information you provide (e.g., name, profile photo)
  • Content you create (e.g., messages, favour requests, help offers)
  • Photos or images you choose to upload when creating or responding to a favour
  • Approximate location data (such as city or area) used to display and discover nearby favours
  • Basic usage data required to operate and improve the app
  • Device information or identifiers used for security and notifications (where enabled)

Location data is used only to enable location-based features and is not continuously tracked in the background.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the app (accounts, profiles, messaging, favours)
  • Display favour requests, photos, and location-based listings within the app
  • Keep the platform secure and prevent abuse
  • Improve features and reliability
  • Send important service notifications (where enabled)

3. Third-Party Services

FAVOUR may use third-party services to operate the app, such as:

  • Firebase Authentication (sign-in)
  • Firestore / Database (storing app content)
  • Firebase Storage (photos, images, and profile pictures)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications, if enabled)

These providers process data on our behalf to provide the app functionality.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers necessary to operate the app, if required by law, or to protect users and the platform (including investigating misuse or fraud).

5. Your Choices

  • Update your profile details in the app
  • Control location permissions in your device settings
  • Control photo and media permissions in your device settings
  • Request deletion of your account and data by contacting us

If you contact us regarding your account or data, we may ask for information such as your email address or user ID to verify your request.

Account deletion information is available here:
https://favour33-app.github.io/favour-privacy/delete-account.html

6. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal requirements, or resolve disputes.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children

FAVOUR is not intended for children under 16.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

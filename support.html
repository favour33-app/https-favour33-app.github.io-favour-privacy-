How can we help?

Contact us if you have a question, experience a technical problem, need help with your account, or want to raise a safety concern.

Email FAVOUR Support

Email: favour.33@hotmail.com

When contacting us

Please include:

  • The email address linked to your FAVOUR account
  • A short description of the problem
  • Your device type, such as iPhone or Android
  • A screenshot where helpful

Please do not send passwords or other sensitive login information.
